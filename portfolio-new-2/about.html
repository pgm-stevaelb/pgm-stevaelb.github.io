I've been a graphic designer since 2013, creating logos and brand identities for small companies. I learned everything by myself using YouTube and other channels as guides. Back then I had a fairly simple background of HTML and CSS.

In 2015 I learned about WordPress and started creating websites using existing themes. This quickly changed when I started using theme builders like Elementor and WP Bakery. Elementor is something I still use today, but my main goto is Oxygen Builder to create stunning themes and carefully finished websites in WordPress. To this day, I still use WordPress to create basic websites.

As I was getting some more attention, I thought I should get a certificate for graphic design. I did a two year course and fell in love, not only with Photoshop and Illustrator, but with InDesign as well. So I got my certificate from Syntra evening school.

Starting in 2021, I'm turning myself into an actual web developer. I extended my knowledge with learning front-end and a bit of back-end development.

I love making websites and apps. And most of all, I love making people happy by helping them with what they want and need.
